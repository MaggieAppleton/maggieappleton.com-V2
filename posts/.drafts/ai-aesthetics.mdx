The founding principles, best practices, leaders, literature, charlatans, communities, figureheads, jargon, touchstones, and taboos are all being negotiated and established within a relatively short time frame. It feels like everyone is racing to write themselves into the history books.



We're pulling on two major, predictable themes:

- Magic and ethereal forces
- Robots and automated machinery
