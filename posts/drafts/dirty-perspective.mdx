Drawing in 3D perspective is one of the biggest technical headaches for anyone who wants to illustrate their ideas.

It can take months just to wrap your head around the basic concepts, and then years to develop an intuitive sense for it and feel proficient.

Some of us don't have time for that BS.

Here's the workaround.

## 1. Download [Blender](https://www.blender.org/download/)

Blender is a 3D modelling tool that's going to solve 80% of this problem for us. It's free. It's open source. And it's astonishingly good given those last two factors.

Install and fire it up.
